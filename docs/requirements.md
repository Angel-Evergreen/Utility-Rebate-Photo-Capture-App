## FR-1 User Intake
Field crews must enter the following before photo upload:
- Crew member name
- Customer name
- City

## FR-2 Photo Capture
Photos must be taken using the in-app camera after intake is completed.

## FR-3 Metadata
Each photo must automatically include:
- Crew member name
- Customer name
- City
- Date and time

## FR-4 Submission Enforcement
Photo submission must not be allowed unless required intake fields are completed.
